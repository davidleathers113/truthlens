🚀 Getting Started

How do I install TruthLens?

Installing TruthLens is super easy! Just visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "TruthLens", and click "Add to Chrome". The whole process takes less than 30 seconds! 🎉

Is TruthLens free to use?

Yes! TruthLens offers a generous free tier that includes basic credibility checking. We also offer premium features for advanced users who need more detailed analysis. 💎

🎯 How It Works

How accurate are credibility scores?

Our AI-powered system achieves 95% accuracy by analyzing multiple factors including source reputation, fact-checking history, and content analysis. We're constantly improving! 🤖

What websites does TruthLens work on?

TruthLens works on all major news sites, social media platforms (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram), and millions of other websites. If it's on the web, we can analyze it! 🌐
